Greenworks Map: Sustainability Projects in Philadelphia

The Mayor’s Office of Sustainability implements Greenworks, Philadelphia’s comprehensive sustainability plan. Greenworks is comprised of 15 measurable targets across five focus areas: energy, environment, equity, economy, and engagement. This Greenworks mapping tool allows users to see sustainability work that has happened in Philadelphia’s neighborhood and to identify opportunities for future work.

Projects displayed on the map reflect the work completed by the City of Philadelphia and its partners from 2009 to 2015, the Greenworks implementation period. For more information about this work and the future of sustainability in Philadelphia, see the Greenworks 2015 Progress Report, available at www.phila.gov/green.
