Philadelphia Reduces Its Environmental Footprint

Environmental stewardship is core to Greenworks. The Environment section focuses on three areas of particular concern: reducing greenhouse gas emissions by 20 percent; improving air quality toward attainment of federal standards; and diverting 70 percent of solid waste from landfill. The following project areas in the Greenworks Map contribute to these targets:

  • Alternative Energy Vehicle Fueling Stations
  • SEPTA Hybrid Bus Depots
  • Air Monitoring Stations
  • Recycling and Waste Management
